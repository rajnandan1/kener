Kener is an open-source status page system designed to help you monitor and communicate the status of your services effectively.

> [!NOTE]
> This documentation is for kener version 4x. There are few changes from version 3.x. Please refer to the [migration guide](/docs/migration/v3-to-v4) if you are upgrading from version 3.x.

## Features {#features}

Here are the top features that make Kener stand out from other status page solutions. Built with modern technologies and designed with both developers and end-users in mind, Kener provides everything you need to monitor, manage, and communicate your service status effectively.

### Open Source & Self-Hosted {#open-source}

Kener is completely open-source under the MIT license, giving you full control over your status page. Host it on your own infrastructure, customize it to your heart's content, and never worry about vendor lock-in or data privacy concerns. You own your data, you control your destiny.

### MIT License {#mit-license}

Kener is licensed under the MIT License, a permissive open-source license that allows you to use, modify, and distribute the software freely. This means you can customize Kener to fit your specific needs without worrying about legal restrictions. Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, Kener's MIT License gives you the freedom to innovate and adapt as your business grows.

### Modern Design {#modern-design}

Experience a clean, modern, and responsive design that looks stunning on all devices. Built with cutting-edge technologies like SvelteKit and TypeScript, Kener delivers a fast, smooth, and delightful user experience. Your status page is often the first place users go when they suspect issuesâ€”make sure it leaves a great impression.

### Powerful Monitoring {#powerful-monitoring}

Monitor your services with confidence using multiple monitor types including API endpoints, Ping checks, TCP connections, DNS records, SSL certificates, and more. Set custom intervals, configure health checks, and get real-time insights into your service availability. Whether you're monitoring a simple website or a complex microservices architecture, Kener has you covered.

### Incident Management {#incident-management}

Create, update, and manage incidents with ease. Keep your users informed with detailed incident reports, status updates, and resolution timelines. Communicate transparently during outages and build trust with your audience by showing you're on top of issues.

### Smart Notifications {#smart-notifications}

Send email notifications to subscribers about incidents, updates, and scheduled maintenance. Configure webhooks, Slack, and Discord integrations to alert your team instantly when issues arise. Set up custom triggers based on monitor status changes to ensure the right people are notified at the right time.

### Maintenance Scheduling {#maintenance-scheduling}

Plan and communicate scheduled maintenance windows in advance. Let your users know when to expect downtime and minimize surprises. Schedule recurring maintenance windows and automatically update your status page when maintenance begins and ends.

### Multiple Status Pages {#multiple-status-pages}

Manage multiple status pages from a single Kener instance. Perfect for agencies, enterprises, or businesses with multiple products and services. Each status page can have its own branding, monitors, and subscriber list.

### Multi-User Collaboration {#multi-user-collaboration}

Work together with your team by inviting multiple users with different roles and permissions. Control who can create incidents, manage monitors, or access sensitive settings. Keep everyone in the loop while maintaining security.

### Complete API Access {#complete-api}

Integrate Kener with your existing tools and workflows using our comprehensive REST API. Automate incident creation, update monitor status, retrieve uptime metrics, and more. Build custom dashboards, mobile apps, or integrations tailored to your specific needs.

### Embeddable Widgets & Badges {#embeddable-widgets}

Integrate status information directly into your own website or application with embeddable widgets. Provide real-time status badges for your services that automatically update. Give your users instant visibility into service health without them leaving your site.

### Customization & Branding {#customization-branding}

Tailor Kener to match your brand identity. Customize colors, logos, favicon, and more. Add custom CSS for advanced styling. Make your status page feel like a natural extension of your product, not a third-party tool.

### Global Reach {#global-reach}

Built-in multi-language support helps you reach a global audience. Display times in users' local time zones for better clarity. Support for multiple locales ensures everyone understands your status updates, regardless of their location.

### SEO Optimized {#seo-optimized}

Search engine friendly pages ensure your status updates are discoverable. Proper meta tags, semantic HTML, and performance optimization mean your status page ranks well and loads fast. When users search for issues with your service, they'll find your official status page first.

### Dark Mode {#dark-mode}

Built-in dark mode provides better accessibility and user experience. Automatic theme switching based on system preferences ensures your status page looks great at any time of day.

### Analytics Ready {#analytics-ready}

Connect external analytics tools like Google Analytics to monitor user engagement and track how users interact with your status page. Gain insights into which incidents drive the most traffic and what information users are looking for.
